Overview

Purpose

White Water Rafting (WWR) is a safe social activity designed to allow groups of outdoor adventures the opportunity to experience life challenges for team work in the safe setting of nature. The business involves small team building activities and youth adventure groups.

This site's purposes are to:

  • Build Revenue : Improve in sales for the company WWR by allowing online bookings
  • Share Information : Improve access to information on the services and products of the WWR company

Audience:

Who are the target customers?
WWR focusses this site content on providing corporate groups information and the accessibility to book small business team building events.
What do they want?

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?
Safety regulations for group bookings (documents for covering group bookings)
Calendar bookings of larger groups that indicate daily availability for exclusive packages to cover team skill building of 5+ individuals to 20 individuals.

What are their needs that aren't being met?
Current sites are focussed on information and small group bookings. No provision of large scale groups or social events are displayed online.

  • Calendar Form Bookings The option for exclusively booking the facilities of the company WWR for a day.
  • Overnight Camping The option for extended site bookings to include facilities and activities beyond a single day event.

How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)?
Most company bookings will be done by means of desktop or laptop devices. A appropriate sized content layout that is adaptable to the mobile device (smart phone) is also needed for directions.
